Multitude
GUI Redesign

This summer (2015) I completed a 3 month internship at The Metropolitan Museum of Art of New York. I worked with the IS&T department on web and UX/UI based projects. Specifically, I was placed with the team of programmers responsible for Multitude and Palladium, The Met's in-house softwares for merchandise and ticketing. Taking into account my background in design, I was tasked with redesigning the interface of Multitude.

I tried something new and coded my comps as interactive web pages rather than still or animated flat-image deliverables. From there, the project expanded into a crash course in Javascript, AJAX, and object oriented design.
